The house is located within the urban area and is distributed in living room, kitchen with pantry and fireplace and natural oak wood furniture to be installed, according to images; 5 bedrooms, bathroom, patio. In addition to the main house, it has other rooms -at different levels- closed and covered that can also be accessed by another street, which would allow its extension and adaptation as another home or using the space as a plot to build another new home, making the pertinent adaptations. Its orientation is southwest and has magnificent views of the Sierra de Alcaparain. In addition, with the previous property a 12,896m2 farm is transmitted. -with the possibility of processing a project for hotel equipment, camping or rural tourism house. The farm is 1.5 km from the town with irrigated and dry land. It has access by paved road and a lane of about 200 meters in length that crosses it. It has a spring to supply a pool that irrigates an orchard with olive trees and vines, although it has the right to water from a catchment in an adjoining stream. The town of Carratraca is located 45 km away on the A-357 road from Malaga, being superbly connected to the cosmopolitan Costa del Sol and the city of Marbella; so the journey by car is covered in just 35 minutes. The study of the future Guadalhorce highway is currently being processed, which will connect it to the Costa del Sol. Carratraca has been a benchmark for health tourism for more than two centuries, although the properties of the sulphurous springs were discovered much earlier. The origin of its famous spa is located in the Roman baths and the Arab baths, which were created in the municipality to take advantage of the medicinal mineral powers of its spring. The town has been declared a Site of Cultural Interest. It is very interesting to visit the spa, which was inaugurated in 1. 855; the mansion of Mrs. Trinidad Grund; the bullring inaugurated in 1. 878 and the church of Ntra. Sra. de la Salud. In its surroundings you can visit the Conde de Guadalhorce reservoir and the gorge with the surprising Caminito del Rey.
